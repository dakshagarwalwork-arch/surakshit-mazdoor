Worker Protection Initiative

Protecting Workers,
Securing Families

Surakshit Mazdoor is an initiative focused on helping informal and industrial workers access low-cost accidental insurance and practical financial protection.

Learn More See Impact

100+

Workers targeted

₹2 Crore

Potential cumulative coverage

₹20

Annual premium per worker

The Challenge

WHAT IS THE PROBLEM?

Many workers remain financially vulnerable to accidents, despite low-cost protection schemes existing.

LOW AWARENESS

Many workers do not know that affordable accidental insurance options exist.

DOCUMENTATION GAPS

Eligibility, paperwork, and bank linkage often become barriers to access.

HIGH FINANCIAL RISK

One accident can deeply affect the income stability of an entire family.

LIMITED SAFETY NET

Workers often remain outside formal financial protection systems.

Our Approach

WHAT IS OUR APPROACH?

We identify workers, assist in enrollment, and help convert awareness into actual protection.

IDENTIFY WORKERS

Map eligible workers and understand their current protection gaps.

ASSIST ENROLLMENT

Support with forms, bank linkage, nominee details, and practical documentation.

ENABLE PROTECTION

Help workers access accidental insurance that can support their families in crisis.

The Scheme

WHAT IS PMSBY?

Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana is a low-cost accidental insurance scheme that provides meaningful financial protection in the event of accidental death or disability.

Premium ₹20 per year
Coverage Up to ₹2,00,000
Type Accidental Insurance

Impact

WHAT IMPACT CAN THIS CREATE?

A very small premium can unlock meaningful financial protection for a large number of workers.

100+

Workers targeted for enrollment

₹2 Crore

Potential cumulative protection enabled

₹2,000

Total annual premium for 100 workers

REAL SECURITY

Improved financial safety for workers and their families

Daksh Agarwal

Founder Motivation

WHY WAS THIS INITIATIVE CREATED?

Through work on Skill Passbook and direct exposure to labour realities, it became clear that workers face not only employment challenges, but also a lack of financial protection. Skilled workers often spend years contributing to industries, yet remain vulnerable to sudden accidents that can affect entire families. Surakshit Mazdoor was created as a practical initiative to bridge workers to affordable accidental insurance and strengthen their safety net.

On-Ground Work

HOW DOES THIS WORK ON GROUND?

Real field implementation makes the initiative credible — from outreach to actual form filling.

OUTREACH

Workers are informed in simple language about why accidental insurance matters.

FORM SUPPORT

Hands-on help is provided for accurate and complete enrollment documentation.

REAL IMPACT

Coverage is not just discussed — it is actually enabled for workers on the ground.
